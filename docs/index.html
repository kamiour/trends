Date: 2023-11-21

The decision, taken by four board members, was shrouded in mystery, with no clear reasons given. This sudden move sparked a wave of resignations, including OpenAI chair Greg Brockman, and senior executives Jakub Pachocki, Aleksander Madry, and Szymon Sidor. The tech community, including luminaries like Eric Schmidt and Brian Chesky, rallied in support of Altman, with major investors advocating for his reinstatement. The company appointed Mira Murati as interim CEO, only to replace her within days with Twitch co-founder Emmett Shear, marking the third CEO change in a brief span. Microsoft swiftly hired both Altman and Brockman for AI initiatives. Almost 500 OpenAI employees threatened to quit, demanding the reinstatement of Altman and Brockman. Steelers Dismiss Offensive Coordinator Matt Canada Amid Season Struggles

Date: 2023-11-21

On November 21, 2023, the Pittsburgh Steelers made a significant move by firing their offensive coordinator Matt Canada. Coach Mike Tomlin announced the decision, highlighting the team's struggle with one of the NFL's worst offenses. The Steelers' performance under Canada's guidance was lackluster, marked by scoring the fifth-fewest points and amassing minimal yardage. Despite a promising start in 2020 as quarterbacks coach and subsequent promotion to offensive coordinator in 2021, Canada's tenure saw the team's offense stagnate. The Steelers ranked poorly in both yards and points during his time, with a notable decline in their passing attack. This season, rookie quarterback Kenny Pickett, despite being a high draft pick, could not uplift the team's performance.
